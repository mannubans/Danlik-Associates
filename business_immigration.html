OUR BUSINESS IMMIGRATION PROGRAMS

BC - Entrepreneur Base Category

To qualify, you must:

  • - Have a personal net worth of at least CAD$600,000;
  • - Demonstrate business and/or management experience;
  • - Establish an eligible new business or purchase and improve an existing business anywhere in British Columbia;
  • - Make an eligible personal investment of at least CAD$200,000;
  • - No IELTS requirement.

BC - Entrepreneur Regional Pilot

To qualify, you must:

  • - Have a personal net worth of at least CAD$300,000.
  • - Demonstrate business and/or management experience.
  • - Establish an eligible new business in a designated city in British Columbia.
  • - Make an eligible personal investment of at least CAD$100,000.
  • - IELTS 4 is required.
LEARN MORE

Owner/Operator Federal Program

You can qualify under this program as a foreign investor entrepreneur by acquiring an established business or launching a new business in Canada, and applying for a work visa as a management employee. To be exempted from the requirements of LMIA, a well laid out business plan demonstrating the positive impact the business will bring to the Canadian economy is required. After a period of approximately 1 year, successful temporary work visa holders can transition to permanent residency under a provincial program or as a federal skilled worker under the Express Entry.
